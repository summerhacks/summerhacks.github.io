SummerHacks

StartupHacks is a 48 hours hacakthon in Timișoara. The judging criteria is 60% technical (implementation, quality of product, design) and 40% business (idea has potential for turning into a business, potential users show interest, ...). Designers, entrepreneurs and, of course, programmers are welcome!

Application process

This is a hackathon for the best only. This is why we require you to fill in a short form in order to register for the hackathon. We will let you know if you're qualified as soon as we can after the applications close. We'll e-mail you with our decision, no matter what it is.

Accommodation, food, toothbrush

Yes, you should bring your toothbrush. Along with a sleeping bag. We'll provide you with a (quiet) place allocated for sleeping at StartupHub Timisoara. There will be food all the weekend. The actual times of meals may be slightly changed during the weekend and there will always be some crisps, sweets and drinks available.

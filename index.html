The weekend

There was free food. There were Vader and Stormtrooper masks. There was a ping-pong table. We’ve had seven teams of very good programmers varying in age from high-school students to professionals and they developed amazing projects. It was terrific.

I hope you all enjoyed the weekend, had a good time, made new connections and, most importantly, I hope you learned something from this experience.

Thank you for coming and see you at next edition or at some other hackathons in either Timișoara, Southampton or anywhere else in the world.

SummerHacks

A 48 hours hacakthon in Timișoara. The judging criteria is 60% tech and 40% business. Programmers, designers and entrepreneurs are welcome!

Accommodation

We will allocate a quiet zone at the venue where participants can take a nap and recharge themselves. Bring a sleeping bag.

Things you need

  • Laptop
  • Toothbrush
  • Sleeping bag
  • Creative hat
  • Magic wand

What to hack?

Anything. This is not an appathon or webathon, it's a hackathon. Hack whatever you want as long as it's pure awesome.
